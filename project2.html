    Event Catalogue

    — Interdisciplinary
          Design: Viewpoint

    The brief for this project was to make an event catalogue based on and covering a design movement in the 20th century the subject
    I chose for the event been the impact of digital technology on the graphic design industry
    the event follows the works of three pioneering designers in early digital design.

    I decided to take a very restrained approach
    to the layout and design of the catalogue
    so as not to clash with the work showcased
    many of which are quite ornate and complex.

    —      Typography       Book Design       Package Design
